System Description

The Used Car System contains information about car models, manufacturers, regions, odometers, etc. Users can not only search for those cars they want by providing some specific conditions, but also can they take a look at some interesting trends. Those trends can help users especially analysts or managers to better understand the sales of the used cars nationwide in recent decades.
